\subsection{\texorpdfstring{\textbf{Summary}}{Summary}}\label{summary}}

Sacha Baron Cohen's character in The Dictator resembles the leader of a
typical authoritarian regime ruling over a poor, less educated
population. This project shows statistically how Singapore, a high
income country with a quality education sector, appears to break this
stereotype with its less than democratic tendencies.

~
